About Us

Grobots: Building Innovators, Leaders, and Creators in Robotics at SRMGPC

Grobots is SRMCEM's premier technical club, dedicated to fostering robotics excellence and technical prowess among students. As the driving force behind the college's robotics events, Grobots is organized into teams of Leads, Coordinators, and Assistant Coordinators to ensure smooth, impactful operations. Beyond technical skills, Grobots instills values like teamwork, professional ethics, and creativity, offering students a platform to share and bring innovative ideas to life.

Each year, Grobots introduces freshers to the basics of robotics through foundational classes, followed by thrilling events during Gantavya, the college's tech fest, where students showcase their practical skills. Our commitment extends to workshops and competitions, enabling students to develop essential skills such as cooperation, sincerity, and adaptability in professional environments.

From seminars and project development to intercollegiate events, Grobots is a hub where students excel in robotics . Join us and experience a journey that's as educational as it is transformative!

Events

Gantavya: SRMCEM's Premier Robotics Event

Organized exclusively by SRMCEM, Gantavya is the ultimate event for robotics enthusiasts, a vibrant gathering dedicated to innovation and technical mastery in robotics. Spearheaded by the Grobots club, this annual event challenges participants to push the boundaries of robotics through thrilling competitions, hands-on workshops, and collaborative problem-solving. Focused on fostering skills in engineering and design, Gantavya provides students a unique platform to transform their technical knowledge into action. From intense robot battles to creative design challenges, Gantavya is where ideas are tested, skills are sharpened, and new solutions come to life. Dive into Gantavya, SRMCEM's signature robotics event, and become part of a community that's building the future, one robot at a time!
